A Way of Life

Amore is a way of life. In the heart of Amore, lies the vision to be More than I am. Amore Fitness is the leading fitness gym in Singapore providing unique state-of-the-art gym equipment specially designed for women. The brand is constantly evolving to encourage self-discovery, seek inspiration and to inspire and find fulfilment in life

Programmes

KickBlitz
Be more explosive and agile with this energetic fusion of kickboxing and High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT). The high-energy music will drive you through intense plyometric, aerobics and strength drills. Boost your endurance and feel powerful as this choreographed workout takes you through heart-pumping sweat-inducing total body conditioning.

StrecthFit
Unique multi-regional stretching exercises and breathing techniques are used in this workout to help you achieve a flexible, strong body and a serene mind. Benefit from increased functional mobility in your daily activities, and allow your breath to bring you into an oasis of calmness.
